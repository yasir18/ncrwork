char *color[6] = {“red”, “green”, “blue”, “white”, “black”, “yellow”};

a. What is the meaning of color?
Ans: color refers to the  array of pointers and it points to the base address of it
b. What is the meaning of (color+2);
Ans: color+2 means pointer to the address of third string

c. What is the value of *color?
Ans: *color means the address of first string

d. What is the value of *(color+2)?
Ans: *(color+2) value is address of third string 

e. How do color[5] and *(color + 5) differ?.
Ans: They differ only in representation.
